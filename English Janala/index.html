Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, it’s free for now. You can learn vocabulary here without any cost.
3. Do I need to create an account?
For now, no account is required. But we will be adding this feature soon!
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by learning a few new words every day, practicing them in sentences, and revising regularly. Reading books, listening to English conversations, and using flashcards or apps are also very effective. Here, you can practice vocabulary step by step and track your progress.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Not right now. But we are planning to launch a Grammar course, and then we will provide an e-certificate for completed courses.
